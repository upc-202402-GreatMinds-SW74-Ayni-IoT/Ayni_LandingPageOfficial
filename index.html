About us

GreatMinds. Currently formed by a group of students from the Peruvian University of Applied Sciences, with the ambition of wanting to help society with our great passion for technology and information technology, we created this startup with the objective of facilitating and improving the production of the agricultural sector in Peru.

Mission: To increase agricultural production with efficiency, safety and transparency among Peruvian producers and exporters.

Vision: the most recognized application in Peru to manage crops in a safe, simple and online way.
